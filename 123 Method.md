This is a communication framework that increases the clarity and structure. When asked a question, take 2-3 seconds to think of the answer decide the main points. The respond with one of...

1. "The main(**one**) reason behind that is..."
2. "It comes down to **two** things:"
3. "The **three** top factors are"
