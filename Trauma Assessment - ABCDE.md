### Airway and Cervical Spine Protection

Check...

- Can patient speak/cry? Airway is fine then
- Look for obstruction - anything in the airway?
- Are they making choking or strange breathing sounds?

Fix...

- Remove visible objects from the mouth
- If they are chocking, using [[Heimlich Maneuver]]
- If not breathing, start [[CPR]]

### Breathing

Check...

- Look: Is the chest moving up and down?
- Listen: Can you hear breathing?
- Feel: Can you feel air from their mouth or nose?
- Is breathing very fast, very slow, or very hard?

If not breathing, start [[CPR]]

### Circulation - Is blood moving in the body? Is there bleeding?

- Is there heavy bleeding?
	- Apply pressure / Use Tourniquet in upper arm / upper leg
- Is the skin very pale, cold, or sweaty?
- Are they feeling weak or faint?
	- Lay the person flat
	- Keep them warm

### Disability - is the brain working

- Are they awake?
	- If unconscious but breathing, place in recovery position
	- Monitor breathing
	- Do NOT give food or drink
- Can they respond if you talk to them?
- Can they respond if you shake or pinch them?
- Are they confused or acting strangely
	- [[Stock Detection - FACE]]

### Exposure - Whole body check

- Any hidden injuries?
- Burns? Swelling? Broken bones?
