### Face

Ask them to smile...

- Is the moth uneven?
- Does one side of face drop?

### Arms

Ask them to raise both arms in front

- Does one arm drift down?
- Can they lift both arms equally?

### C - Speech

Can they say a simple sentence(Repeat after me "Today is a sunny day")

- Is speech slurred?
- Are they using wrong words?
- Can they talk at all?

### Emergency

If any of the above sign is yes...

- Call emergency service right away
- Note time of symptom starting
- Keep person upright if awake
- Do NOT give food or drink
- Stay with them
- Even if symptoms go away, its still serious
