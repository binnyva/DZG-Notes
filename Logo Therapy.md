Viktor Frankl made/ refined Logo Therapy during his stay in the concentration camps - and documented that experience in his book 'Man's search for meaning'.

Its one of the three Vienna schools of psychology - others being...

- [[Psycho-Analytic Theory|Fraudian School]] / Will to Pleasure
- [[Adlerian Psychology]] / Will to Power

People ultimate search is for meaning. Suffering becomes much easier to bear if you attach meaning to it.

> He who has a 'why' to live for can bear with almost any 'how'.
> Friedrich Nietzsche
