If the patient is unconscious and not breathing, start CPR. Steps...

1. Check safety - make sure the area is safe for you and the patient
2. Check response - tap/pinch shoulders, shout 'are you ok'. Be a bit violent. They should not be able to ignore you
3. Call for help - Call emergency service - or ask for someone nearby to call. Ask for AED/Defibrillator
4. Check Breathing
	- Put your ear to their mouth while looking at their chest
	- Listen/feel for breath - look for chest going up/down
	- Wait 10 seconds
5. Start Chest Compressions - if not breathing, start CPR
	- Place person on the flat of their back
	- Kneel next to their chest
	- Place heel of one hand in the center of chest
	- Other hand on top
	- Lock your elbows - they shouldn't bend
	- Push using body weight
	- Push 5-6 cm deep - based on body type
	- 100-120 pushes per minute
	- Let chest come fully up before pushing
	- Rotate with other people if you have to
6. Do not stop until
	- Medical person tells you to stop
	- Person starts breathing normally
	- You are exhausted
